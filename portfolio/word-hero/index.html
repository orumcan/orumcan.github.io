Word Hero

January, 2021
1 min read

Word Hero is the unique, fun and free online multiplayer word game. You will enjoy this simple yet challenging word game. Challenge your brain while having fun. Place letters and make meaningful words.

Word Hero is a turn based word game where each player has a unique board. Take turns calling out a letter. As each letter is called out, you write the letter on your board. The goal is to make as many meaningful words as possible. When all the board is full, both boards are disclosed and who has the most meaningful words wins.
